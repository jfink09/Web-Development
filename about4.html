This Is QuestUni


Our Mission is to get more undergraduate students involved in research.


Undergraduate research opens many doors for students' futures. Students involved in undergraduate research have the opportunitiy to be creative, solve new problems,
make new discoveries, and contribute to the greater good of humanity.





QuestUni was founded in 2020 by three Penn State students in the College of Engineering who wanted to improve the structure of how students can learn about and become connected to undergraduate research. The main way that QuestUni wants to accomplish this is throught a different approach than most universities - by providing students with an online platform where they may be helped with their search for research opportunities based on their interests and career goals.

There is no real structure students can use to find a research opportunity. Typically, students have to do a lot of searching, write many emails, and try to talk with many professors to even see what exists at their university. If a professor does not go to a student in class and ask if they are interested in research, looking for a project or a lab can bring great friction. No matter how much a student knows about faculty's research, they may not even get a response from them by email. There needs to be an easier way for students to learn about research and the opportunities that exist at their university, and there needs to be an easier way for students to be connected to research through a structured process tailored to themselve.

Students tend to have a wide variety of topics about which they are passionate that often deviates outside the boundaries of their major, and therefore their department. This causes many students to find it difficult to be exposed to all of the research opportunities at their school. QuestUni developed a research form that retrieves pertinent information about the student and their interests and goals. The goal for this is to show students as many different research projects and labs in which they may be interested in collaborating and show students opportunities based on their responses that is offered at their school with respect to research. When students use this form, they will find projects that suit their interests and skills faster than before, and find other undergraduate, graduate or faculty working on a given project as a team with whom the student may network.

To achieve our goals, QuestUni is proactive in developing useful research content that students can view on a daily basis to improve research awareness, what is possible through research, and tips on how to find research. QuestUni posts interviews with students who are involved in research and professional scientists, engineers, and physicians who became successful through performing research. When students collaborate and innovate at their school, they discover, learn and gain experience to help progress their community and the world further.

This is only the beginning. As QuestUni expands its features and users, it also expands more opportunities for students to network, collaborate, and become more involved with their peers and school. QuestUni continues to try and bridge any gaps in the ability for students to become their best selves before they graduate and go on to lead their careers. The future we want, is a future where students find what drives them to do amazing things in their careers and a future that maximizes the opportunities for students to get involved in things that motivate them while in their college careers.
