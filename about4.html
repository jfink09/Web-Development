This Is QuestUni


Our Mission is to get more undergraduate students involved in research.


Undergraduate research opens many doors for students' futures. Students involved in undergraduate research have the opportunitiy to be creative, solve new problems,
make new discoveries, and contribute to the greater good of humanity.

Founded At The Pennsylvania State University


QuestUni helps students get connected to undergraduate research labs and projects more easily. QuestUni helps students navigate around the difficulties of the unstructured process students experience when searching for research opportunities.



Of 9,610 students had stated they had not participated in formal research

Many students think undergraduate research is only for students with the intention of attending graduate school or an internship is more useful. QuestUni helps reduce this misconception by generating content about what can be accomplished by performing undergraduate research.


A Data Buddies survey of 9,610 college students was done to understand why such a small amount of students participate in research. The largest reasons were due to thinking internships are more important, or not knowing the opportunities exist. https://cra.org/cerp/data-buddies/.

Why Us?


- We are Penn State graduates.

- Our main goal is to assist students in finding projects that help them reach their career goals.

- In our time at Penn State, we witnessed the lack of awareness and difficulty finding the right project.

- Collectively, our team has several years of research experience and know its inner workings.
